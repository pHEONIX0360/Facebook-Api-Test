Facebook Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Facebook, your privacy is our priority. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality, integrity, and security of your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and share your data across Facebook services.

Information We Collect

Facebook collects data to provide better services to all our users. The data we collect includes:

  • Information you provide, such as personal details when signing up.
  • Data about your interactions on the platform, including posts, comments, and shared content.
  • Device and network information for technical purposes.

How We Use Information

We use the data we collect to:

  • Improve the functionality of our platform and personalize your experience.
  • Provide relevant ads and sponsored content.
  • Maintain the safety and integrity of our services.

Sharing Information

Facebook may share your information with:

  • Third-party service providers who help us operate and improve our services.
  • Advertisers and partners for personalized advertising.
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies as required by law.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system is completely secure, and we encourage you to take appropriate precautions when using our services.

Your Rights

You have control over your data. You can:

  • Access and update your information in your account settings.
  • Request the deletion of your data in accordance with applicable laws.
  • Manage privacy settings to control who can view your content.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our privacy practices, you can contact us at Facebook Privacy Support.
